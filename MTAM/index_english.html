About CCBB

Based in the Tancredo Neves edifice, CCBBs main objective is making the access to different
cultural productions available to the citizens of Brasília. Five galleries, movie theaters,
gardens, performance stages, gathering spaces and open in-natura town squares are only some
of the services CCBB has to offer — not to mention the cafes and bistros. The museum also
counts with accessibility measures such as reserved seats for PwDs, Libras interpreters and trained teaching artists.
