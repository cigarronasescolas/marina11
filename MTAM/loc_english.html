CCBBs Van

CCBB offers a free transportation service. All it takes is waiting at the pickup
place or buying a ticket to save a seat on the van. The van takes off at a bus
stop near the Biblioteca Nacional, headed to CCBB. The trip lasts for about one hour
with no stops. You can also take a trip back to the Biblioteca with the van.
Boarding times are scheduled:

Near the Biblioteca Nacional headed to CCBB: 12PM, 2PM, 4PM, 6PM, 8PM

CCBB headed to the Biblioteca Nacional: 1PM, 3PM, 5PM, 7PM, 9PM.
